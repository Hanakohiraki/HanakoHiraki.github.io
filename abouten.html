The Story Behind TWIPg

The beginning of limitless imagination

Our Mission

At TWIPg (The World Impact group), we believe that everyone has their own world of imagination. Our mission is to build a bridge between the real world and those worlds through a mix of media including music, stories, animation, and Virtual World technology to deliver new and memorable experiences.

Our Journey

This project was born from a group of friends who are passionate about Japanese pop culture and Isekai (another world) stories. We started by composing music and writing short stories to share within our small group, until our dream grew into a determination to make the "Rhythm Isekai" universe a reality.

Our Philosophy

  • Creativity First: Creativity is the heart of our work.
  • Community Driven: The voice of everyone in the community is our driving force.
  • Borderless Imagination: We believe imagination has no boundaries.
